Guidelines for Establishing New Partnerships

  1. Identify potential partner institutions aligned with our strategic goals
  2. Conduct initial discussions to explore mutual interests and benefits
  3. Develop a detailed proposal outlining the scope of the partnership
  4. Review and approval process by relevant committees and leadership
  5. Draft and sign a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU)
  6. Implement the partnership activities and monitor progress
  7. Regular evaluation and renewal of the partnership
